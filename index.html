About Us

Lease Hub is a one-stop destination to lease two-wheeler bikes for delivery riders. We provide delivery riders with the option to lease the bike they need to deliver food, groceries, and package goods. We offer the best deals in the market and that's why we have a fleet of 2,000+ bikes ready to be leased in and around your city. We've made it our mission to provide you with the latest and most updated two-wheelers available. Backed by extensive experience and expertise in the industry, our clients benefit from our best practices and lessons learned, while we work relentlessly to keep our fleet of bikes well maintained and delivered on time.
Our mission is to build a platform that's not just about bikes — we're also creating an ecosystem of businesses that deliver products. We're solving some of the biggest problems in e-commerce by connecting local businesses with people who need their services. At Lease Hub, we understand the flexibility required for delivery riders to use two-wheelers in your city. That’s why we offer all-inclusive packages that include bike, helmet, and insurance with every lease agreement. By partnering with us, you will get competitive rates as low as 2.5% per month for a period of 2 years (some conditions apply).
